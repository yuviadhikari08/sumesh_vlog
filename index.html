About Me

The person behind the lens

📍 Uttarakhand, India

I'm Sumesh, the face behind TheSumesh_Vlogs — a travel vlogger and lifestyle creator based in the mountains of Uttarakhand. What started as a way to remember my own journeys has grown into a small community of people who travel through my eyes every week.

I believe the best stories are unscripted — a sunrise over the ridgeline, a conversation with a stranger who becomes a friend, the quiet between two big moments. My work lives at the intersection of travel, storytelling and craft.
